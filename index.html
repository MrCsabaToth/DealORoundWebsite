Welcome to Deal-O-Round's webpage

A fun card game featuring poker combinations with a hint of tetris and slot machines. Find poker combinations on the table and gain points! Give us feedback on our Facebook page. This game started as a contestant for the 59DaysOfCode competition by Team Bad Wolf. Our goal is to provide you an opportunity to have a good time!
